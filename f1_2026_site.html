2026 Season Story

The 2026 season opened a brand new chapter with sweeping power unit regulations — a 50/50 split between ICE and electric power — plus active aerodynamics, reshuffling the entire competitive order. Mercedes emerged as the benchmark, with 19-year-old Kimi Antonelli delivering four consecutive victories to dominate the early championship.

Reigning champion Lando Norris and McLaren have struggled to replicate 2025 dominance. Ferrari keep Hamilton and Leclerc within striking distance. Red Bull showed recovery signs with Verstappen's first podium in Canada.

Brand-new constructors Audi and Cadillac join for 2026, expanding the grid to 11 teams and 22 drivers for the first time in modern F1.
